The Bavarian Army Museum

The Bavarian Army Museum is one of the largest military history museums in Europe, housed within the magnificent New Castle (Neues Schloss) in Ingolstadt. Founded in 1879, the museum offers a comprehensive journey through Bavarian military history from the Middle Ages to the present day.

What makes this museum particularly remarkable is its balanced approach to military history. Rather than glorifying war, it presents a critical examination of military conflicts and their impact on society. The museum's collections include impressive displays of armor, weapons, uniforms, and historical artifacts that tell the story of Bavaria's military past.

Don't miss the World War I exhibition in the Reduit Tilly, which provides a sobering look at the realities of modern warfare. The museum's location in the 15th-century Gothic castle adds to the atmospheric experience, making it a must-visit for history enthusiasts and casual visitors alike.

Audi's Heritage in Ingolstadt

Ingolstadt and Audi share a history that dates back to the post-World War II era. While Audi's roots are in Saxony, the company found its new home in Ingolstadt in 1949 when the Auto Union GmbH was reestablished here. Today, Ingolstadt is synonymous with Audi's global headquarters and main production facility.

The Audi Museum Mobile, located next to the factory, takes visitors on an exciting journey through automotive history. From the historic Auto Union racing cars to the latest Audi models, the museum showcases the brand's commitment to innovation, design, and engineering excellence. The museum's circular architecture symbolizes the continuous development of automotive technology.

Beyond the museum, Audi's presence has shaped Ingolstadt's modern identity. The company is the city's largest employer and has contributed significantly to its economic growth and international recognition. The Audi Sportpark, home to FC Ingolstadt 04, stands as another testament to the company's deep integration into the city's cultural and social life.
