Rich History from the Medieval Ages

Founded in 806 AD, Ingolstadt boasts a remarkable history as a former Bavarian capital and fortified city. Walk through the beautifully preserved Old Town where medieval architecture tells stories of centuries past. Don't miss the iconic Kreuztor - the stunning 14th-century gate that serves as the city's symbol.


Automotive Heritage

As the headquarters of Audi, Ingolstadt is a paradise for car enthusiasts. Visit the impressive Audi Museum to explore German engineering at its finest and discover the evolution of automotive innovation.


Culture and Tradition

Experience Ingolstadt's vibrant cultural scene with numerous museums, theaters, and annual festivals. The city beautifully blends its medieval roots with modern energy, offering something for every visitor.


Jewel of Bavaria

Situated in the heart of Bavaria, Ingolstadt provides easy access to the Romantic Road and other German treasures, making it an ideal base for exploring southern Germany.
